The Best Movies According to Shashank

My top 7 movies of all-time.

The Gods must be Crazy

A duology, i.e. a collection of 2 movies. Also, two of the most beautiful movies I have ever watched. Takes you completely away from the hustle of the modern life, into the existential reality of life, through the eyes of the Kalahari bushmen.

Titanic

I am not a big fan of romantic movies. However, this is one my all-time favorites. Truly Spectacular Cinema!!

Shawshank Redemption

A True Masterpiece of Cinema that has really stood the test of time.

Jurassic Park

One of the greatest Sci-Fi movies ever made. The Gold Standard of Science Fiction.

The Terminator

A sci-fi classic that never fails to TERRIFY and also entertain!

Inception

Incredibly complex, challenging to watch, but great fun when you understand what the hell is going on. A visionary piece of art (could've been a little more visionary, but no complaints :D )

Avatar

Probably one of the most visually stunning movies ever made, aside from the Marvel stuff, of course.
